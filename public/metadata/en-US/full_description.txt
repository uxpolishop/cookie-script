## About

Comply with GDPR and CCPA through simple integration with Cookie Script services. Create your own pop-up of cookie information without the need for programming skills. The service can be used free of charge.

## Features

- Check, categorize and add description to all cookies found on your site automatically;
- Get access to detailed reports and analysis on all types of cookies used;
- Have all visitors' consents stored so that you can download them, according to GDPR;
- Allow your users to suspend the consent of cookies at any time on your page;
- Allow the Cookie Script to delete all cookies until the visitor agrees with the Cookie Policy and blocks third-party cookies;
- Select which cookies are strictly necessary (functional) for your website to function properly.
